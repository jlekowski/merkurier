We would like to offer you our services in the field of international transportation involving the usage of small, and medium trucks ( from 1 to 15 tons load ). We have been on the market since 1993, and have a wide experience in this field.
We have various types of vehicles including: canvas, trucks trailors, boat carriers, and solo trucks. Our solo trucks have the capacity to carry loads ranging from 3 to 5 tons, the truck with the trailer can carry up to 15 tons. The total volume of the truck and trailer amounts to 90m3. The solo trucks have the total mass capacity of 7,5 tons, and therefore, makes it possible for us to travel even on Saturdays, Sundays, and Holydays, on the entire European territory, thanks to this we are in the position to deliver and pick up cargos at any designeted point in Europe, at a quicker speed.
Our trucks are in possesion of an international license and our activity is secured with international transport insurance with a liability amount to 100 000 USD.
We have signed contracts with frontiers shipping companies, therefore, there is no need to be concern about transit authorization to the place of unloading.
All of our drivers are in posession of mobile phones, in addition our vehicles are equipped with GPS transmitters used for tracking shipments, in order to assure the safety of your merchandise.
